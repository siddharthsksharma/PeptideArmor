FAQS

You can purchase lab-grade research peptides from reputable suppliers like Peptide Armor. We operate under ISO-certified conditions, ensuring ≥98% purity and strict adherence to research-use-only protocols. Our peptides are shipped in temperature-controlled packaging to maintain integrity.

Research peptides are short chains of amino acids used exclusively for laboratory research purposes. They are not intended for human consumption and must be handled according to strict research protocols.

Look for suppliers that provide third-party lab testing results, operate under ISO certification, offer transparent purity documentation, and maintain clear research-use-only policies.

Key factors include purity levels (≥98%), third-party testing certificates, proper storage and shipping protocols, clear labeling, and a commitment to research-use-only standards.

Research peptides are available for purchase by qualified researchers and institutions. They are sold for laboratory research purposes only and are not intended for human consumption.

Research peptides should be stored in a refrigerator at 2-8°C, protected from light, and kept in their original packaging. Proper handling includes maintaining cold chain integrity during shipping and storage.

Popular research peptides include BPC-157, TB-500, GHRP peptides, GLP-1 agonists, and various growth hormone-releasing peptides used in metabolic and regenerative research.

Shipping policies vary by supplier and destination country. Please check with your supplier regarding international shipping availability and any applicable regulations.

Shipping times depend on your location and selected shipping method. Most orders are processed within 1-2 business days and shipped via temperature-controlled carriers to ensure product integrity.
